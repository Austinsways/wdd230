About Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods is a company based in Carlsbad, California that produces high-quality organic fruit and handmade fruit drinks. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy and nutritious food, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible products.

Our Mission

At Bountiful Foods, our mission is to promote health and wellness by producing delicious and nutritious organic fruit and fruit drinks. We believe that by choosing organic and sustainable agriculture practices, we can help protect the environment and support local farmers.

Our Products

Our organic fruit is grown using only the highest quality, non-GMO seeds and sustainable farming practices. We carefully handpick each piece of fruit at the peak of ripeness to ensure the best flavor and nutrition. Our handmade fruit drinks are made with organic fruit, filtered water, and a touch of natural sweeteners like honey or maple syrup. We never use artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives.
