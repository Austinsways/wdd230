Albertsons Stadium

Albertsons Stadium is home to Boise State Universitys Broncos! The Blue and Orange field is an iconic look, and does an amazing job of communicating the spunk and creative minds behind boise's builders.

Boise Capitol Building

The Boise Capitol Building is exactly that! It's home to the government of the State of Idaho. Its construction started in 1905 and its longlasting structure is a great representation fo the quality behind Boise's builders.

Idaho Old State Penitentary

The Idaho old State Penitentary was built in 1870 and is open to the public to see what the prison was like in the past. It was functional from 1872 to 1973.

Boise Train Depot

The Boise Train Depot is a historic, Spanish style structure that provides a meeting place for people around Boise. It Provides Beauiful high up views of Boise.

Zions Bank Building

The Zions bank building is one of Boise biggest Buildings! It owned by zions bank and is a funtional office for its employees. It's large size shows the growth of Boise's community.
