Target Audience:

The target audience is contractors looking for work and builders looking to post work to be done.

  • 20-50 years old

  • Significant construction experience in a specific field

    • Goals/Tasks
  • Get job done quickly and effeciently
  • Stay within timeframe
  • Make money off of project
    • Environment
    Typically contractors are suited to one or two profession for example insulator or drywaller or sheetrocker etc.

    Scenarios:

  • Where can I find local contractors for stucco?
  • Who will pay me for digging a foundation?
  • Are there any contractors in (area) for drywall?
  • Are there any builders hiring contractors nearby?
  • Which contractors are the busiest?
  • What market is lacking builders/contractors?
