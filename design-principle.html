Design Principles Document

Austin Eldredge

Alignment: NASA

This page does a good job of showing the alignment principle because all the elements are centered and move with the screen. They also have consistency with the locations of there sections

White Space: Zillow

Zillow offers plenty of white space throughout the page to make it feel like its totally uncluttered. This helps the user not to feel overwhelmed and have confidence navigating the page, or highlighting the elements they want the user to navigate towards.

Contrast: Github

Github does a great job of showing contrast in text and photos within their home page. The white text on purple/black background makes it easily readable.
